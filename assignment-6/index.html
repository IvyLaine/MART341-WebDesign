Guide to Wheel Throwing

Tools and equipment

  • 1 Clay
  • 2 Spongs
  • 3 Water
  • 4 Trimming tools
  • 5 Bucket

Directions

  1. Wedge clay on canvas covored table to get rid of air pockets
  2. Push clay down firmly in center of wheel making sure it is secure
  3. Apply water to clay with sponge and turn on wheel not too slow
  4. Mold hands around clay with elbows supported on legs at a 90oF and center clay on wheel
  5. Once centered an indentation with your thumb can be made in center to start forming desired object
